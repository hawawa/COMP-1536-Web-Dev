ABOUT THE CAFÉ

Here you will find rich coffee roasts, artisan teas, buttery flaky pastries and delicious gourmet sandwiches and soups. Marina’s love for colourful, confetti-like French macarons inspired our jewel box collection, featured alongside other frivolities. Our food is skillfully prepared by Marina and her talented team.


Ideal for a breakfast bite, your morning coffee, a sumptuous lunch or afternoon snack attack, Marina Café ensures efficient table service alongside a grab-and-go selection of house-made confections, sandwiches and light bites. Specialty coffees are custom roasted locally by 49th Parallel and served by a team of highly trained baristas.


Find a hand-chosen selection of flavorsome, fresh, savory and sweet offerings from the kitchen of Marina. Whether it’s a quick bite over custom coffee blends on the way to work or a refreshing light lunch after a visit to the shops or nearby art gallery, Marina Café is sure to delight and inspire.
